Turkish Chicken Kebabs

Description

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of Turkish cuisine with these mouthwatering Turkish Chicken Kebabs. This recipe captures the essence of traditional Turkish flavors, combining tender chicken pieces with a harmonious blend of spices and herbs. With its vibrant colors and tantalizing aroma, this dish is sure to transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Istanbul. Whether grilled to perfection or cooked on skewers in the oven, these Turkish Chicken Kebabs are a delightful culinary adventure.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut the boneless, skinless chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes, ensuring they are clean and trimmed of excess fat.
  2. a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, ground paprika, ground coriander, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to form a marinade.
  3. Place the chicken cubes in the marinade, ensuring they are evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or transfer the chicken and marinade into a resealable bag. Let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes or ideally overnight.
  4. Preheat your grill or oven to a high broil setting.
  5. If using skewers, thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, leaving a small gap between each piece for even cooking. Alternatively, you can cook the chicken directly on the grill or in a grill pan.
  6. Grill or broil the kebabs for about 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a lightly charred exterior. Check for doneness by cutting into a piece to ensure it's no longer pink in the center.
  7. Transfer the Turkish Chicken Kebabs to a serving platter and garnish with fresh parsley.
  8. Serve the kebabs as a main dish with rice pilaf, grilled vegetables, or a fresh salad.
  9. Optional: Add a dollop of tzatziki or squeeze some lemon juice over the kebabs for added flavor.
  10. Gather your loved ones, and enjoy the delicious taste of Turkey with these irresistible Turkish Chicken Kebabs.
